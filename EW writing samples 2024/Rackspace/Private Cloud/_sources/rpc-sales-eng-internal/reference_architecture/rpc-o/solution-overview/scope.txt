=====
Scope
=====

This document describes the system, components, and configurations,
required to meet the general needs necessary to host cloud ready
applications.

Infrastructure as a Service:

-  Stable, performant, scale out solution

-  Strong security & operational controls over the environment

-  On-going support and management

-  Physical and logical separation to handle Prod and DR

-  Self service capabilities

-  Rapid deployment of compute, object, and storage resources
