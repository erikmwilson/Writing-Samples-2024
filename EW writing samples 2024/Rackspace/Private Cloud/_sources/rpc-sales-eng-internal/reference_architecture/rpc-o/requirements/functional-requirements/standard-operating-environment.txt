Standard operating environment
==============================

   :widths: 30 70
   :header-rows: 1

   * - **Section/Requirement**

   * - SOE3.0
     - The system shall provide a standard operating environment
       including:
   * - SOE3.0.1
     - The system is based on Ubuntu 16.04 LTS.

       **Solution:** Running KVM based hypervisors, we implement
       Ubuntu as the host OS.
   * - SOE3.0.2
     - The system shall provide effective documentation about
       operational procedures.
   * - SOE3.0.3

       place with teams assigned to define this.
