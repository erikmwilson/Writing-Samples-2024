====================
Load Identity access
====================

Use the following steps to load Identity access:

1. Open ServiceNow.

#. In the left-hand navigation pane, click **Identity Access**.

#. On the **Identity Access** screen, click **New**.

#. Complete the form and click **Submit**.

   The user should be someone who currently has access to MyRack. Multiple
   users can be created.
