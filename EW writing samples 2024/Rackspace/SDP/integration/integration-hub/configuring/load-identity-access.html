Load Identity access#

Use the following steps to load Identity access:

  1. Open ServiceNow.

  2. In the left-hand navigation pane, click Identity Access.

  3. On the Identity Access screen, click New.

  4. Complete the form and click Submit.

    The user should be someone who currently has access to MyRack. Multiple users can be created.
