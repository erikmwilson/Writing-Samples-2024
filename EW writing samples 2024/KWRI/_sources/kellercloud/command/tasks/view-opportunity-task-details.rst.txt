View Opportunity Task Details
=============================

To view Opportunity task details, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click the **Opportunity Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **Show my task for** drop-down, select the time range.

#. Click the **Contact Associated** name to view the Contact details.

#. On the Contact details page, click the **Tasks** tab.
