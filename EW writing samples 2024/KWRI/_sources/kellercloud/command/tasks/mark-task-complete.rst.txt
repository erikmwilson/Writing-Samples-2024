Mark Task as Complete
=====================

Once you have completed a task, click the empty checkbox to the left of the task to mark it as complete.

To mark a task as complete, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click either the **Contact Tasks** tab or the **Opportunity Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. For Contact tasks, click **To Do**. Only tasks in this status can be marked as complete. Click the empty checkbox next to the correct task. On the pop-up window, indicate if you were able to reach the client and add any **Notes**. Click **Complete Task**.

#. For Opportunity tasks, click the empty checkbox next to the correct task. On the pop-up window, click **Complete Task**.
