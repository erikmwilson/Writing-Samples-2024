Create a Contact Task
=====================

To create a Contact task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click the **Contact Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. Click **Create New Task**, at the top-right of the page.

#. On the **Create Custom Task** window, enter the following information:

   * **Task Name**

   * **Due Date** Uncheck the **All Day** checkbox, to select the time of day for your due date.

   * **Task Description** (optional)

   * **Recruit(s)** attached to this task (optional)

   * **Hyperlink** that you need to complete the task (optional)

#. Click **Associate Contacts**, to add specific contacts to the task (optional). On the **Associate Contacts** window, Use the search to find the contacts. Click the Add Contact icon |add-contact-icon| for each contact you want to add. After you have chosen all of the contacts to add to the task, click **Assign Contacts**.

   .. |add-contact-icon| image:: ../../../images/add-contact-icon.png
      :height: 30

#. Click **Create Task**.
