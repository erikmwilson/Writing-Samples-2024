View Contact Task Details
=========================

To view contact task details, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click the **Contact Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **Show my task for** drop-down, select the time range.

#. Click the status of tasks to display, **To Do**, **Completed**, **Archived** or **Expired**.

#. Click the task name to view the task details.
