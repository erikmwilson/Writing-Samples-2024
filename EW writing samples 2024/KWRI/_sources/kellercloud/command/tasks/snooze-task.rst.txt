Snooze a Task
=============

If you need more time to complete a task, you can snooze (reschedule) the task.

To snooze a task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click either the **Contact Tasks** tab or the **Opportunity Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. For Contact tasks, click **To Do**. Only tasks in this status can be snoozed.

#. Click the snooze icon |snooze-icon|.

   .. |snooze-icon| image:: ../../../images/snooze-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Snooze this task** window, choose to **Snooze interval in days** or **Snooze until specific date** and click **Snooze**.
