Delete a Task
=============

Only Recruit pipeline tasks can be deleted.

To delete a task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-login.rst

#. Click either the **Contact Tasks** tab or the **Opportunity Tasks** tab, at the top of the page.

#. For Contact tasks, click **To Do**. Only tasks in this status can be deleted.

#. Click the delete icon |delete-icon|.

   .. |delete-icon| image:: ../../../images/delete-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the confirmation window, click **Yes, delete**.
