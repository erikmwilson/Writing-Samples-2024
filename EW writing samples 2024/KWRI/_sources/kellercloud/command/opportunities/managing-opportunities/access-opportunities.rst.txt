Access an Opportunity
=====================

After you have creating Opportunities, you can access through Contacts or Opportunities.

Access Opportunities Through Opportunities
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. include:: ../opportunities-login.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Opportunities** |opportunities-icon|.

   .. |opportunities-icon| image:: ../../../../images/applet-opportunities.svg
      :height: 20

#. Click on a phase to see your Opportunities or :ref:`search-opportunities-command`.

   .. note::

      When accessing or searching for a Lease Opportunity, make sure you choose the correct selection in the **Tenant** drop-down (either Tenant or Landlord).

Access Opportunities Through Contacts
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

.. include:: ../opportunities-login.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. In the **Contacts** list, click the name of the contact.

#. Click the **Opportunities** tab, at the top-right of the contact record.

#. Select an Opportunity.
