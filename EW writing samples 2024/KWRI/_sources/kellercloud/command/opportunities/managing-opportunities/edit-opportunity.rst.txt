Edit an Opportunity
===================

As your Opportunity moves through your pipeline, you may have to update the information.

To edit an Opportunity, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../opportunities-login.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Opportunities** |opportunities-icon|.

   .. |opportunities-icon| image:: ../../../../images/applet-opportunities.svg
      :height: 20

#. Click on the phase (Cultivate, Appointment, Active, Under Contract, Closed) that contains the Opportunity.

#. Click on the Opportunity Name to view the Opportunity details.

#. Click the Edit |edit-icon| icon for the section you want to update.

   .. |edit-icon| image:: ../../../../images/edit-icon.png
      :height: 20

   **General Information**

   This is the main details section of any Opportunity and includes fields that are relevant to the type of Opportunity you are working with.

   **Property**

   This section displays information about the attached listing.

   For listing Opportunities, you attach a listing from MLS. For buyer Opportunities, you attach a listing to each offer you create. After you attach a listing or accept an offer with a listing attached, you can edit the Property section to edit any of those listing details that pulled in from the IDX feed.

   **Seller Worksheet (Listing Opportunities Only)**

   This section is used to give your seller more financial information to access when comparing offers.

#. When complete, click **Save**.
