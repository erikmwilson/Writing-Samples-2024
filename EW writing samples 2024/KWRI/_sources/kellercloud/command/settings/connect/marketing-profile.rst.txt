Marketing Profile
=================

To create a marketing profile, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Connect Settings**.

#. Click **Marketing Profile**.

#. Fill out the sections of the marketing profile with your information. You can upload your photo and logos.

#. To use this information to brand your agent site, select **Use my information to brand my agent site**.

#. When complete, click **Save**.

.. note::

   For a step-by-step marketing profile walkthrough, click **Marketing Profile  Kelle Guide**.
