
.. _contact-archive-settings-command:

Contacts Archive
================

To restore of permanently delete Contact archives, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Contacts** to display the Contacts settings.

#. Click **Contact Archive**.

#. To restore an individual Contact archive, click  **Restore** in the correct row.

#. To permanently delete an individual Contact archive click the Delete |delete-icon| icon in the correct row and click **OK** in the confirmation box.

   .. |delete-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/delete-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. To restore or delete multiple Contact archives select one or more archive check boxes:

   .. note::

      To select all Contact archives, use the check box in the header row.

   a. To restore multiple selected Contact archives, click  **Restore Selected**.

   #. To permanently delete selected Contact archives, click **Delete Selected Permanently** and click **OK** in the confirmation box.
