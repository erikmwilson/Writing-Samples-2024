Database Wipe
=============

To archive all Contacts or delete all Contact archives, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Contacts** to display the Contacts settings.

#. Click **Database Wipe**.

#. To archive all contacts, click **Archive All Contacts** and click **OK** in the confirmation box.

#. To permanently delete all archived Contacts, click **Delete All Archived Contacts** and click **OK** in the confirmation box.
