Landing Page
============

To change the look of your landing page, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Agent Sites**.

#. Click **Landing Page**.

#. Select the look of your landing page from the drop-down box.

#. Click **Save**.

#. To preview your landing page, click **Preview LP**.

#. To preview an email using this new look, click **Preview Email**.
