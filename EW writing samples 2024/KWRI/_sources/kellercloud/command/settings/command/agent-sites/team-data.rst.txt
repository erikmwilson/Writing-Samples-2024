Team Data
=========

To determine which team members to show on your neighborhood landing page, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Agent Sites**.

#. Click **Team Data**.

#. Enter the name of your team and a team summary.

#. Select the team members to show on your landing page.

#. Click **Save**.
