My Logs
=======

To view a history of imported contacts and download log files, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Import Logs**.

#. Click **My Logs**.

   The My Logs page displays a history of all imported contact files.

#. To download the original imported contact file, click **Download** in the **Original File** column.

#. If an imported contact list had errors, you can download the error file by clicking **Download** in the **Error File** column.
