Design Template
===============

Choose the Sketchouse template to use to send offers to your clients and co-broker agents by completing the following steps:

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Opportunity Settings**.

#. Select the default Sketchouse template to use to send offers to your clients and co-broker agents and click **Save Settings**.
