Team Stats
==========

To view lead routing statistics for your team, complete the following steps.

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **Command Settings** to display the
   Command settings.

#. Under **Command Settings**, click **Lead Routing**.

#. Click **Team Stats**.

#. Select the correct team in the header.

#. The **Claims** view (default), shows the number of leads given and claimed by each team member.

#. Click **Routes** to view detailed route information for each team member.
