Access Command
==============

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

   Click the eye icon to view your password as you enter it.

   .. note::

      If you have forgotten your password, click **Forgot Password** to
      reset your password.

      If you are unable to recover your password using the **Forgot Password**
      link, you can contact KW Support at support@kw.com.

#. Click **Sign In**.
