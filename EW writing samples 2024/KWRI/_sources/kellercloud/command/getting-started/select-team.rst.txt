Select your KW team
===================

.. note::

   You must be part of a team to access this feature.

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. On the top-right of the Command interface, click **Select Team**.

#. Select your team from the drop-down menu.
