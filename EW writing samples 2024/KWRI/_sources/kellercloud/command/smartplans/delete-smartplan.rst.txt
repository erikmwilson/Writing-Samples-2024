Delete a SmartPlan
==================

You cannot delete an active SmartPlan (one containing contacts). You must first :ref:`unsubscribe-contacts-command`.

To delete a SmartPlan, complete the following steps:

.. include:: smartplans-login.rst

#. Click the **MySmart Plans** tab, at the top of the page.

#. Find the correct SmartPlan in the list and click the menu options icon |menu-options-icon| and select **Delete**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 30

#. On the confirmation pop-up, click **Yes, delete**.
