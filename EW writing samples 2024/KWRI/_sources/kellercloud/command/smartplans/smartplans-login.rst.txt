
.. _access-smartplans-command:

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. On the top-left corner, click **Command**.

#. On the left toolbar, click **SmartPlans** |smartplans-icon|.
