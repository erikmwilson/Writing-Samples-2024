Share a Contact (Teams)
=======================

To share a contact with a team member, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#.  Locate the appropriate contact, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Share Contacts**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Share Contacts** window, select the team you would like to share with, the specific agents you would like to share with, and if those agents can edit the contact's information.

#. Click **Share**.

   The contact is sent to the agents that were selected and is visible under their Shared Contacts.
