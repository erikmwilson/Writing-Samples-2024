Add Contact or Lead (Teams)
===========================

For Teams, Rainmakers have the ability to manually assign Contacts and Leads to Team Members.

To assign a Contact or Lead, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Select your Team from the **Select Team** drop-down at the top of the page.

#.  Locate the appropriate contact, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Assign To**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Set Claim** window, select the Team Member to assign the lead to, and click **Assign**.

   The lead appears in the assignee's contact list and the contact record shows it has been claimed by the assignee.
