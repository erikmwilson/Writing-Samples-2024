More Options Menu
=================

Many of the tasks needed to manage your contacts can be accessed from the Contacts list without opening an individual contact record.

The More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) is in the last column of each contact in the Contact list.

.. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
   :height: 20

Click the icon for the following options:

* :ref:`edit-contacts-command`

* :ref:`add-activity-contacts-command`

* :ref:`share-contacts-command`

* :ref:`add-note-contacts-command`

* :ref:`archive-contacts-command`

* :ref:`view-smartplans-contacts-command`

* :ref:`add-smartplans-contacts-command`

* :ref:`mark-unmark-lead-contacts-command`
