Add Multiple Contacts to Email List
===================================

To add multiple contacts to your Mailchimp email list, complete the following steps:

.. note::

   Your must have a connected Mailchip account. For more information, see :ref:`connect-application-settings-command`.

.. include:: contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Select the check boxes next to the contacts you to add to your email list. To select all contacts, select the check box in the header row.

   .. note::

      You can filter your contact list using Filters to target certain criteria (for example, **Primary Address**).

#. Click the **Select Bulk Action** drop-down menu and select **Add to Email List**.
