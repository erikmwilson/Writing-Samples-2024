Add a Contact
=============

With Command, you can add individual contacts or import multiple contacts at one time from a database such as a CRM or contact list (for example, Google Contacts).

For more information on importing contacts, see :ref:`Import Contacts <import-contacts-command>`.

To add an individual contact, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Click **Add Contact**, at the top right of the page.

#. In the **Add to Contacts** window, enter your contact's information. To enter additional information when creating the contact, click **Add More Information** and additional sections appear. For more information, see :ref:`contacts-info-fields-command`.

#. Click **Create**, to add the contact.
