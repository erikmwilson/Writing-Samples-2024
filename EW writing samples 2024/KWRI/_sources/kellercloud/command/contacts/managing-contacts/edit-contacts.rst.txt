Edit a Contact
==============

As you are developing and maintaining your contact database, you may need to update or edit some existing information in a contact record.

To edit a contact, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#.  In the **Contacts** list, click the name of the contact you want to edit.

#. Click the Edit |edit-icon| icon next to the contact's name to access the **Edit Contact** window. If you wish to enter in additional information when creating the contact, click **Add More Information** and additional sections will appear with fields within each of those sections.

   .. |edit-icon| image:: ../../../../images/edit-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Edit or add any necessary contact information. For more information about the contact fields, see :ref:`contacts-info-fields-command`.

   You can also add custom fields to meet the needs of your business and how you prefer to grow relationships with your clients. For more information about custom fields, see :ref:`custom-fields-settings-command`.
