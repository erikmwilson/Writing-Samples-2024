View SmartPlans
===============

To view a contact's SmartPlans, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Locate the appropriate contact, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **View SmartPlans**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. The **SmartPlans Quickview** window displays all the SmartPlans for this contact.
