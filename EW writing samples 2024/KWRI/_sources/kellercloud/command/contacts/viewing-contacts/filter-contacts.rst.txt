Filter Contacts
===============

In Contacts, you can use filters so you can find the contacts you need. To filter contacts, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Click **Filters** above the contact list.

#. In the  **Filters** window, select the filters you want to use from available options and the drop-down lists. To remove a filter, click on each filter to remove it individually or click **Clear All** to remove all of the selected filters.
