Customize Columns in Contacts
=============================

In Contacts, you can customize columns and change the order in which they display contact information. To customize columns in contacts, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../contacts.rst

#. On the left toolbar, click **Contacts** |contacts-icon|.

   .. |contacts-icon| image:: ../../../../images/contacts-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Click **Customize Columns** at the top of the page.

#. In the  **Customize Columns** window, select the columns that you want to display and the order in which they display.

#. Click **Apply**.

   The customized column selections are saved for the current Smartview you are viewing.

.. note::

   You can also drag the boundary of the columns (in the header row) to adjust their width.
