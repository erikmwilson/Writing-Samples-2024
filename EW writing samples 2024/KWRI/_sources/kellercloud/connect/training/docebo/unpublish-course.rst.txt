Unpublish a Course
==================

To remove a course from Training, complete the following steps:

1. From a browser, log in to Docebo using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. Click the gear icon in the top right corner to access the **Admin Menu**.

#. Click **Courses Management** from the **E-Learning section**.

#. Click the **Name** of the course you want to unpublish.

#. Om the course page, click on the **Properties** tab.

#. At the bottom of the page, click the **Status: Published** menu and select **Under Maintenance**. Click **Save Changes**.

   The Course is available for users in Training.
