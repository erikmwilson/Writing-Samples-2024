Filter Training
===============

While searching training courses, you may want to display courses by type or status.

To filter training, complete the following steps:

.. include:: training-login.rst

#. Click on the area of training you want to filter:

   * Library

   * My Bookshelf

   * Channels

#. Click the filter icon |filter-icon| at the top of the page.

   .. |filter-icon| image:: ../../../../images/filter-icon.png
      :height: 30

#. When the filters fly-out menu appears on the left side of the page, select filters by clicking the checkbox beside them. To remove filters, you can either remove them individually or click **Clear Filters**.

   You can filter by **Type** (On Demand, Learning Path, Channel Resource) or **Status** (Not Started, In Progress, Done).
