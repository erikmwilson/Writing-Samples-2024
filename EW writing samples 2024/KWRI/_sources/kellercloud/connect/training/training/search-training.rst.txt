Search Training
===============

To find the correct On Demand Course, Learning Path, or Channel Resource to complete your training, you can search the training Library, your Bookshelf, and the training channels.

To search training, complete the following steps:

.. include:: training-login.rst

#. In the **Search Library** field, enter your search term.

#. The **Search Results** page lists all matches for your search term. The number of results are shown for each of the following areas:

   * **All Results**

   * **My Bookshelf**

   * **Course Content**

   * **Channel Resources**

   Click one of the above tabs to list the search results for that that area.
