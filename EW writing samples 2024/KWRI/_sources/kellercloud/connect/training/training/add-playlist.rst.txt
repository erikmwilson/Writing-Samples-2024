Add Training to Playlist*
=========================

Playlists are used to organize content within a channel. You can create your own playlists and keep them private or share them with others. Playlist also show up as a custom filters depending on what channel you are viewing.

To add training to a playlist, complete the following steps:

.. include:: training-login.rst

#. Search for the On Demand Course, Learning Path, or Channel Resource. For more information, see :ref:`search-training-connect`.

#. Click the training name.

#. On the course page, click the **Add to Playlist** button (circle with a plus sign).
