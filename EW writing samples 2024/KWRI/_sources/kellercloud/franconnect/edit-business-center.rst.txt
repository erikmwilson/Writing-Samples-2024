Edit a Business Center
======================

After a new Business Center has been added to FranConnect, you can edit it in FranConnect. For more information, see :ref:`add-organization-franconnect`.

To edit a Business Center, complete the following steps:

.. include:: franconnect-login.rst

#. Click on the **Market Center Number** or **Entity Name** to edit that Market Center.

#. On the **Market Center Info** page, click **Business Center** from the **Market Center Info** column menu.

#. On the **Business Center Information** page, click **Modify** for the correct Business Center.

#. Edit the Business Center information at necessary. The following fields have some special considerations:

   * **Business Center** - The Business Center name.

   * **State** - Select a state from the pre-defined list. WorldWide Business Centers so not have a state value.

   * **Country** - Select a country from the pre-defined list. WorldWide Business Centers so not have a country value.

   * **Legal Close Date** - This value becomes the End Date and Legal Close Date in the KW AMS database. Original Open Date in the KW AMS database is kept empty.

   * **Address 2** - This field does not sync with the KW AMS database. Anything entered here is available only in FranConnect.

#. Click **Save** at the bottom of the page,
