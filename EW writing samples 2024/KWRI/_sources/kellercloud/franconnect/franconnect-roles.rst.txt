CommandMC Access by Role
========================

The following roles can access CommandMC.

Regional Access
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* Regional Operating Principal

* Regional Director

* Regional MCA

* Regional Operations Manager

* Regional Staff

* Regional Director Assistant

* Regional MCA Assistant

* Area Director

* MAPS Regional Leadership Coach

* Regional Tech Trainer

Market Center Access
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* Market Center Administrator (MCA)

* Assistant Market Center Administrator (AMCA)

* Market Center Leader

* Operating Principal

* Team Leader

* Assistant Team Leader

* General Manager

* MC Tech Coordinator

* Productivity Coach

* MC Broker

MC Team Management Access
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

* MCA

* AMCA

* KWRI System Administrators

* KWRI Legal
