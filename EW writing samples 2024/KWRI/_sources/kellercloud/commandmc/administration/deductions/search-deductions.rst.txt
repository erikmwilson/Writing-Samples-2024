Search Deductions
=================

While you are managing your deductions in CommandMC, you may need to find a specific deduction.

To search deductions, complete the following steps:

.. include:: admin-mc-login.rst

.. note::

   If you have Regional and Market Center roles or have leadership roles in multiple Market Centers, click your name, at the top-right of the page, and make sure the correct Market Center profile is selected.

5. Click **My Market Center**.

#. In the **My Market Center** section, click **Deductions**.

#. Enter the search information for the desired deduction. After you locate the deduction in the list, click the deduction ID to view the deduction details.
