.. note::

   KWRI Support Tools are available only to certain KW roles. For more information, see :ref:`command-mc-roles-command-ug`.

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. On the top-right corner of Command, click your name.

#. Select **KWRI Support Tools** from the drop-down menu.
