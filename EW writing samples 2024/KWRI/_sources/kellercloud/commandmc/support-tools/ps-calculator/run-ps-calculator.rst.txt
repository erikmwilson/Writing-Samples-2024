Run Profit Share Calculator
===========================

To run the Profit Share Calculator, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../support-tools-login.rst

#. On the left-hand Supprt Tools pane, click **PS Calculation**.

#. On the **Calculator** tab, click **Let's Go**.

#. The tool calculates your Market Center data, click **Next to Associates**.

   .. note::

      To repeat a step, click **Recalculate**. If a step returns a **Failed** message, click **Restart Calculation**. (**Are there troubleshooting steps to fix a failed calculation?**)

#. The tool calculates your Associate data, click **Next to Profit Share**.

#. The tool calculates your Profit Share data, click **Validate & Create Reports**.

  Validated reports display on the **Calculator** tab.
