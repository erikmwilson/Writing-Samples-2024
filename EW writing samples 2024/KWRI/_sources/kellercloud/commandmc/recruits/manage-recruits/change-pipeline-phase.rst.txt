Change the Recruit's Phase or Stage
===================================

You change the position of a recruit in the Pipeline from the recruit list.

To change the position of a recruit in the Pipeline, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruit Management** tab, at the top of the page. A list displays of all your Market Center recruits.

#. Search for the correct recruit. For more information, see :ref:`search-recruits-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that recruit and select **Change Pipeline Phase**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Move to another stage** window, select a new **Phase** or **Stage**.

#. Click **Save**.
