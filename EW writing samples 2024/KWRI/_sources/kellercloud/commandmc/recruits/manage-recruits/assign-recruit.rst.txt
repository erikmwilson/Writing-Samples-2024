Add or Edit an Assignee for a Recruit
=====================================

To add or edit an assignee to a recruit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruit Management** tab, at the top of the page. A list displays of all your Market Center recruits.

#. Search for the correct recruit. For more information, see :ref:`search-recruits-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that recruit and select **Assign** or **Edit Assignee**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Add Assignee** window, select the assignees. You can select more than one. To remove an assignee, click the X next to the assignee name.

#. Click **Assign**.
