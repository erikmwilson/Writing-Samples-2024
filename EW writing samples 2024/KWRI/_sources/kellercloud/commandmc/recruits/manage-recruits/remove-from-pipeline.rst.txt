Remove Recruit from Pipeline
============================

If the recruit you are working with falls through for any reason, you can remove them from the pipeline. The recruit record remains in the Recruit database.

To remove a recruit from the pipeline, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruit Management** tab, at the top of the page. A list displays of all your Market Center recruits.

#. Search for the correct recruit. For more information, see :ref:`search-recruits-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that recruit and select **Remove from Pipeline**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Remove from Pipeline** window, select **Fall out**.

#. Enter a removal comment in **Why are you removing this recruit?**. The recruit is returned to the recruiting database for further engagement.

#. Click **Save**.
