Customize Columns in Recruits
=============================

To customize columns in Recruits, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruit Management** tab at the top of the page.

#. Click the settings icon |settings-icon| to the right of the column headings.

   .. |settings-icon| image:: ../../../../images/settings-icon.png
      :height: 25

#. On the **Customize Columns** pop-up, check the column headings you want to display and drag them into the correct display order.

#. Click **Apply**.
