Connect to BrokerMetrics®
=========================

To automatically pull recruit production information from BrokerMetrics, you must connect your Market Center with BrokerMetrics.

.. note::

   You must first :ref:`bm-create-account-recruits-mc` through the KW Marketplace.

To connect your Market Center with BrokerMetrics, complete the following steps:

.. note::

   CommandMC settings are available only to certain KW roles. For more information, see :ref:`command-mc-roles-command-ug`.

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. On the top-right corner of Command, click your Market Center.

#. Select **Settings** from the drop-down menu.

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **CommandMC Settings** to display the
   CommandMC settings.

#. Click **Applications** and then **BrokerMetrics®**.

#. On the **BrokerMetrics®** page, click **Connect Account**.

#. On the **Connect BrokerMetrics®** window, enter your **Username** and click **Connect**.

   You receive a **BrokerMetrics® Account Connected** confirmation message.
