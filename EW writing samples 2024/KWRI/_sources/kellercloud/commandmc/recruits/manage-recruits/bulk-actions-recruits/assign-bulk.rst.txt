Assign Multiple Recruits to a Market Center
===========================================

You can assign multiple recruits to a Market Center using the Bulk Action feature.

To assign multiple recruits to a Market Center, complete the following steps:

.. include:: recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruit Management** tab.

#. Select the check boxes next to the recruits you want to assign to a specific Market Center. To select all recruits, select the check box in the header row.

   .. note::

      You can filter your recruits list using Filters to target certain criteria (for example, **Pipeline Status**).

#. Click **More Options** under the recruits list and select **Assign**.

#. On the **Add Assignee** window, select the assignees. You can select more than one. To remove an assignee, click the X next to the assignee name.

#. Click **Assign**.
