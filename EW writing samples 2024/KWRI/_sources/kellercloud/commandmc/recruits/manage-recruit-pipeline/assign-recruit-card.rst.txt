Add or Edit an Assignee for a Recruit
=====================================

To add an assignee for a recruit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../recruits-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruiting Pipeline** tab, at the top of the page. Your recruit cards are organized by Phase, Stage, and the currently selected Sort pattern.

#. Search for the correct recruit. For more information, see :ref:`search-pl-recruits-mc`.

#. Click **Assign** |assign-icon| to assign an new assignee, or the picture of the current assignee.

   .. |assign-icon| image:: ../../../../images/assign-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. Check or uncheck the required assignees and click off the window to close.
