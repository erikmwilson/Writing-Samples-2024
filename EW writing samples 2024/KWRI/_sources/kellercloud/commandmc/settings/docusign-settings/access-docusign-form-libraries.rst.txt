Access Market Center Form Libraries in DocuSign
===============================================

The Form Libraries in the DocuSign Market Center Admin account consist of every form you would find in each MLS. These libraries include all of the forms from the MLS.

You can create a form groups for each transaction type and association to make it easy for your agents to access the forms they need.

For more information, see :ref:`create-docusign-form-groups-commandmc`.

To access your Market Center Form Libraries in DocuSign, complete the
following steps:

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **CommandMC Settings** to display the
   CommandMC settings.

#. Click **DocuSign Settings**.

#. Select your **Market Center** from the drop-down and click **Go to Docusign**.

#. A DocuSign pop-up appears with your Market Center's **klrwxxx@kw.com** email address populated, click **Continue**.

#. Enter your password on the next page and click **Log In**.

#. On the KW DocuSign Dashboard, click the **Profile** icon on the top-left corner and select **Admin** from the drop-down.

#. On the next page, click the **Forms** tab.

#. You can access your Market Center Form Groups from the **Forms** tab.
