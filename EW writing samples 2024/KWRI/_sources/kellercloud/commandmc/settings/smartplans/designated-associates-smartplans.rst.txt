Select Designated Associates
============================

You can set the default designated associates for the following SmartPlan step types:

* Tasks

* Email

* SMS/MMS

To select the designated associates, complete the following steps:

.. include:: ../settings.rst

#. On the left-hand Settings pane, click **CommandMC Settings** to display the
   CommandMC settings.

#. Click **SmartPlans**.

#. On the **Designated Associates** page, select your Market Center from the drop-down list.

#. From the **Tasks** drop-down, select the default assignee for the tasks. This can either be the recruit assignee or Market Center leadership.

#. From the **Email** drop-down, select who the emails should come from. This can either be the recruit assignee or Market Center leadership.

#. From the **SMS/MMS** drop-down, select who the messages should come from. This can either be the recruit assignee or Market Center leadership.

#. Click **Save Changes**.
