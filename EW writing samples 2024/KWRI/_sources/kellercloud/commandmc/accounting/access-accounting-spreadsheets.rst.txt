Access Accounting Reports
=========================

To access Accounting reports, complete the following steps:

.. include:: accounting-mc-login.rst

#. Click one of the following tabs:

   * **Balance Sheet** - Select the correct month in the **Select Month** drop-down.

   * **Income Statement** - Select the correct month in the **Select Month** drop-down.

   * **Account History**

#. To print the report, click the print icon |print-icon|.

   .. |print-icon| image:: ../../../images/action-print.svg
      :height: 30
