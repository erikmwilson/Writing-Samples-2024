
.. _access-associates-commandmc:

.. note::

   CommandMC is available only to certain KW roles. For more information, see :ref:`command-mc-roles-command-ug`.

1. From a browser, go to: https://console.command.kw.com.

#. Log in using your Keller Williams user name and password.

#. On the top-left corner, click **CommandMC**.

#. On the left toolbar, click **Associates** |associates-icon|.

   .. |associates-icon| image:: ../../../images/associates-icon.png
      :height: 30

   A list displays of all your Market Center associates.
