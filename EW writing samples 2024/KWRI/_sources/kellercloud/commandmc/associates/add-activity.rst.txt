Add an Activity
===============

CommandMC tracks the activities of the associate (in person meetings, calls, or texts) in the associate's timeline.

To add an activity to an associate, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Search for the correct associate. For more information, see :ref:`search-associates-commandmc`.

#. Click the associate name to view the associate details.

#. On the **Associate Details** window, click the **Timeline** tab.

#. Click **Add Activity**.

#. On the **Add New Activity** window, enter the following information:

   a. Choose an **Interaction Type** using the drop-down:
      * Meeting
      * Call
      * Text

   #. Select the date of the activity. Click in the **Date** field and choose from the calendar widget.

   #. For calls or texts enter the **Outcome**.

   #. Enter a **Description**, or note about the activity.

#. Click **Save**.

   You are now able to track all of the notes and activities for your associate, in the associate's timeline.
