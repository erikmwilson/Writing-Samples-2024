Add a License to an Associate
=============================

To add a license to an associate, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Search for the correct associate. For more information, see :ref:`search-associates-commandmc`.

#. Click the associate name to view the associate details.

#. Click the **Business** tab.

#. In the **Real Estate Licenses** section, click **Add a license**.

#. On the **Add License Information** window, enter the **License Number**, **State/Province**, and **Expiration Date**. You can add and remove multiple licenses.

#. Click **Save**.
