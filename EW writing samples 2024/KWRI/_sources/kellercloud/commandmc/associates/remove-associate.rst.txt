Remove Associate
================

To remove an associates from the Market Center, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Search for the correct associate. For more information, see :ref:`search-associates-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that associate and select **Remove Associate**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Remove Associate** window, select the **Departure Actual Date** and **Reason of Departure**.

#. In the **Send Information to** section, enter the **Email** of anyone who should be notified and any **Additional Notes**.

   .. note::

      You can add multiple email addresses by hitting enter after each email is entered.

#. Click **Save**.

#. On the **Confirm Departure** window, click **Confirm**.

   If the departure date is in the future, the Associate status is changed to **Leaving**. If the departure date is in the past, the Associate status is changed to **Inactive**.

   If the Associate has a departure date in the future and then decides to stay, you can open the **Remove Associate** window and click **Cancel** any time before the departure date.
