Add Existing KW Associate
=========================

To add an exisitng KW associate to your Market Center, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Click **Add an Associate** and select **Add Existing KW Associate**.

#. On the  **Add an Existing Active Associate** window, enter the **Associate's KWUID**, and click **Add Associate**.

#. On the **Associate Confirmation** window, verify that the associate information is correct, and click **Confirm**.

#. On the **Required Info to add Associate** window, indicate if the associate has a real estate license and if they should have access to CommandMC, and enter the associate's **National ID**.

#. Click **Add Associate**.

   The associate is added to your Market Center.
