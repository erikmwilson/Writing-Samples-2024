Search Associates
=================

While you are managing your associates in Command, you may need to find a specific associate. You can search associates based on the first and/or last name of the associate.

To search associates, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Enter the search information for the desired associate. After you locate the associate in the list, click the associate name to view the associate details.

To save these results as a Smartview, see :ref:`smartview-associates-commandmc`.
