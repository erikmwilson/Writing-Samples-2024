Activate Agent
==============

To activate an agent, complete the following steps:

.. include:: associates-mc-login.rst

#. Search for the correct associate. For more information, see :ref:`search-associates-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that associate and select **Activate Agent**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Activate Agent** window, enter the **License Number**, **State/Province**, and **Expiration Date**. You can add and remove multiple licenses.

#. Enter the **Company Dollar Split (Agent)**, **Company Dollar Cap**, **Next Period Company Dollar Cap**, and select the **Company Dollar Anniversary Date**.

#. Click **Activate Agent**.
