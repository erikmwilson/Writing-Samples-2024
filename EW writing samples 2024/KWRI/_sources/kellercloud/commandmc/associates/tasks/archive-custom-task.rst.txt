Archive a Custom Task
=====================

To archive a custom task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Select **Other Tasks** from the drop-down.

#. Locate the appropriate task, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Archive**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the confirmation window, click **Archive**.
