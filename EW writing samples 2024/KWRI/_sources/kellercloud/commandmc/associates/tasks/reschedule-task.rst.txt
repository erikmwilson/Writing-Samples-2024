Reschedule Task
===============

To reschedule a task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Select either **Checklist Tasks** or **Other Tasks** from the drop-down.

#. Select **To Do** from the drop-down. Only tasks in **To Do** status can be rescheduled.

#. Locate the appropriate task, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Reschedule**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Reschedule Task** window, enter a date interval or select a specific date and click **Reschedule**.
