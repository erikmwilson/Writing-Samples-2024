Create a Custom Task
====================

To create a custom task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. Click **Other Tasks**.

#. Click **Create Task**, at the top-right of the page.

#. On the **Create Custom Task** window, enter the following information:

   * **Task Name**

   * **Due Date**

   * **Assignee** - The assignee for this task. You can only select one assignee.

   * **Task Description** (optional)

   * **Recruit(s)** - The recruits attached to this task (optional).

   * **Hyperlink** - Any hyperlink that you need to complete the task (optional).

#. Click **Create Task**.
