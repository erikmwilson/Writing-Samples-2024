View Checklist Task Details
===========================

You can view checklist task details from the **Recruits** tab.

To view checklist task details, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **View Tasks** drop-down, select either **My Tasks** or **All MC Tasks** to display only your tasks or all Market Center tasks.

#. Click **Checklist Tasks**. A list displays of all the checklist tasks.

#. Click the status of checklist tasks to display, **To Do** or **Completed**.

#. Search for the correct task. For more information, see :ref:`search-tasks-commandmc`.

#. Click the task name to view the task details.

#. To mark the task as complete, click **Complete Task**, otherwise click **Cancel** to close the details window.
