View Custom Task Details
========================

You can view custom task details from the **Recruits** tab.

To view custom task details, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **View Tasks** drop-down, select either **My Tasks** or **All MC Tasks** to display only your tasks or all Market Center tasks.

#. Click **Other Tasks**. A list displays of all the custom tasks.

#. Click the status of custom tasks to display, **To Do**, **Completed**, **Archived** or **Expired**.

#. Click the task name to view the task details.

#. To mark the task as complete, click **Complete Task**, otherwise click **Cancel** to close the details window.
