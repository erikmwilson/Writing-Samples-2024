Search Tasks
============

While you are managing your tasks in CommandMC, you may need to find a specific task. You can search tasks by the task name.

To search tasks, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **View Tasks** drop-down, select either **My Tasks** or **All MC Tasks** to display only your tasks or all Market Center tasks.

#. Click **Checklist Tasks** or **Other Tasks**.

#. Click the status of tasks to display, **To Do** or **Completed**.

#. In the search bar, enter the search information for the desired task.
