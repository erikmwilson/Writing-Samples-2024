Delete a Task
=============

Only Recruit pipeline tasks can be deleted.

To delete a task, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **View Tasks** drop-down, select either **My Tasks** or **All MC Tasks** to display only your tasks or all Market Center tasks.

#. Click **Checklist Tasks**. Task in both **To Do** and **Completed** statuses can be deleted.

#. Locate the appropriate task, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Delete**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the confirmation window, click **Delete**.
