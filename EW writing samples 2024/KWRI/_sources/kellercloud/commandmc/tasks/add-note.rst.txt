Add a Note
==========

CommandMC tracks all notes related to the recruit.

To add a note to a recruit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: tasks-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Recruits** tab, at the top of the page.

#. From the **View Tasks** drop-down, select either **My Tasks** or **All MC Tasks** to display only your tasks or all Market Center tasks.

#. Click either **Checklist Tasks** or **Other Tasks**.

#. Click the status of tasks to display, **To Do**, **Completed**, **Archived**, or **Expired**. The task must have an attached recruit.

#. Locate the appropriate task, click the More Options menu (ellipses |menu-options-icon| icon) and select **Add Note**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Add New Note** window, enter a **Note Title**, **Description**, and click **Save**.

  You can now access this, and other notes that you add, on both the recruit's timeline, and on the recruit details **Notes** tab.
