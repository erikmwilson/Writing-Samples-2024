View a Deposit
==============

You can view the details of deposits for the current period.

To view a deposit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Deposit** tab at the top of the page.

#. Click the **Deposit History**.

#. Find the correct deposit and click the deposit number to view the deposit details in the **Deposit Journal Entry** section.
