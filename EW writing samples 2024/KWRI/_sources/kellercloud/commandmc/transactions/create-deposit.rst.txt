Create a Deposit
================

After closing a transaction, the Market Center must then distribute the funds to the recipients (check or ACH) and deposit the commissions checks from the closing entity.

To create a deposit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Deposit** tab at the top of the page.

#. On the **Create Deposit** tab, select one or more transactions in **Outstanding Transactions** section.

#. Click **Create Deposit**.

   .. note::

      To preview the deposit in PDF format, click **Preview Deposit**.
