Delete Transaction
==================

You can only delete transactions in **Open** status.

To delete a transaction, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Transaction** tab at the top of the page:

#. Search for the correct transaction. For more information, see :ref:`search-transactions-commandmc`.

#. Click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that transaction and select **Delete**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Delete existing transaction?** window, click **Yes, Delete**.
