Search Listings
===============

While you are managing your listings in Transactions, you may need to find a specific listing.

To search listings, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Listings** tab, at the top of the page. A list displays of all your Market Center listings.

#. Enter the search information for the desired listing. After you locate the listing in the list, click the listing to view the listing details.
