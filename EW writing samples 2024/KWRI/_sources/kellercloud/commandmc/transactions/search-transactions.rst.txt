Search Transactions
===================

While you are managing your transactions in CommandMC, you may need to find a specific transaction.

To search transactions, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Transaction** tab, at the top of the page. A list displays of all your Market Center transactions.

#. Enter the search information for the desired transaction. After you locate the transaction in the list, click the transaction to view the transaction details.
