Delete a Deposit
================

You can delete a deposit in the current period.

To delete a deposit, complete the following steps:

.. include:: transactions-mc-login.rst

#. Click the **Deposit** tab at the top of the page.

#. Click the **Deposit History**.

#. Find the correct deposit and click the ellipses icon |menu-options-icon| (on the far right) for that transaction and select **Delete**.

   .. |menu-options-icon| image:: ../../../images/menu-options-icon.png
      :height: 20

#. On the **Delete existing deposit?** window, click **Yes, Delete**.
